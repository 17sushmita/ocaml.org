Supporting and Getting Support

The current development of OCaml is lead by Inria, and commercial support is provided by the OCamlPro company.

The Caml Consortium at Inria

The Caml Consortium federates the design and development of the Caml language and its programming environment. The Consortium allow its members to demonstrate their interest in the Caml language and express their support to its development. Moreover, they benefit of a specific license.

To join the Caml Consortium, companies pay an annual fee of 3000 euros. If you are interested, follow this link.

Commercial Support by OCamlPro

Commercial support can be provided by OCamlPro. OCamlPro provides different services, from simple support to advanced development in OCaml, and training and consulting.
